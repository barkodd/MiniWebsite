Black Templars: Painting Guide

In this tutorial, we're going to discuss the fine art of slapping black paint on grey plastic.

This painting guide has two parts - one without using an airbrush, and one with it.
Techniques shown here can easily be applied on any black armour-clad miniatures, such as Chaos Warriors or the Deathwatch.
I also explain how to paint details like gold, robes, and purity seals, which are pretty universal.
